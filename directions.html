Directions to Make Coconut Pineapple Cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the cake flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Gradually add the egg whites and mix until well combined.
  5. Mix in the sour cream and coconut extract.
  6. Add the flour mixture in three parts, alternating with the coconut milk, beginning and ending with the flour mixture. Stir until just combined.
  7. Fold in the shredded coconut and pineapple juice.
  8. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Once cooled, spread a layer of pineapple curd on one cake layer.
  12. Place the second cake layer on top and frost with cream cheese frosting.
  13. Press flaked coconut onto the sides of the cake for decoration.
  14. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
